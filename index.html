Hello!

Are you looking for a hassle free way to hire top tech talents? We can help with that. We have a vast network of prescreened IT professionals, with varying levels of seniority that will fit seamlessly into your project.

Our team is fully staffed by previous and current IT professionals so we can 100% understand your needs. How do we work? First, we understand your project (what you are building or trying to build and technology stack) then we get a picture of your organisation's culture. We then match you with one or more of our prescreened professionals.

We are London based and can supply your with: staff on your site anywhere in the Uk, off site staff based on our UK campus or remote staff based in Spain or Africa. We are one of few UK tech recruitment companies with strong network in Africa and Spain.
