What we do

We are specialists in Agile consulting and software Quality Assurance.

Agile Consulting

Our team of experts are at hand to help you on your agile transformation journey. We have years of experience in implementing and refining agile processes.

Quality Assurance

Do you worry about something going wrong when you deploy changes to production or think the quality of your product can be improved? Then let us handle your quality assurance needs. We will not only ensure that your products function correctly but also make sure that there aren't any quality gaps in the software development process. We are experts in API, Web and Mobile applications testing.

Test Automation

Today's fast paced, quick feedback environment means we can no longer rely on manual testing alone. At West Global, we love test automation. We will help you select the right tools, implement a robust test automation framework and train your staff, so that you can continue using the framework long after we are gone.

Outsourced Testing

Whether you are looking to outsource your entire testing function or need external resources to compliment your in-house team, West Global can help. We have highly competent onshore/offshore testers that will fit in seamless into your team. This means you are able to get resources on demand without the need to hire, train or fire employees.
